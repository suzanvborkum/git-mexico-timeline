MIGUEL HIDALGO CRIES
THE GRITO DE DOLORES

Miguel Hildalgo cries the Grito de Dolores

On September 16 1810 priest Miguel Hidalgo speaks to a crowd in Dolores Hidalgo and says the famous words: ¡viva la Virgen de Guadalupe!, ¡viva Fernando VII!, ¡viva la América y muera el mal gobierno! These words marked the beginning of the independence.

18 10

EXECUTION MIGUEL HIDALGO

Execution Miguel Hidalgo

After some small successes with his followers in among other Guanajuato, Miguel Hidalgo and his fellow rebel leaders are captured. On July 30 1811 Hidalgo is stripped of his priesthood, found guilty, and executed.
